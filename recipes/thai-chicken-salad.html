Thai Chicken Salad

Description

This Thai Chicken Salad recipe is always the perfect dish for a special (but easy) dinner. Weâ€™re celebrating here today and, true to form, my celebration food is savory and involves soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Napa cabbage thinly sliced (about 4 cups)
  • 1/4 small red cabbage thinly sliced (about 2 cups)
  • 2 medium carrots grated (about 1 cup)
  • 3 green onion thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup minced cilantro
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken breast
  • 3 tablespoons slivered almonds toasted

The Dressing

  • 1 lime juiced
  • 3 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 teaspoons agave nectar or honey
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 2 teaspoons rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chili garlic sauce

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Napa cabbage, red cabbage, carrot, green onion, cilantro and chicken breast. Toss with the dressing. Garnish with the toasted almonds. Serve.
  2. In a small glass bowl, combine the lime juice, peanut butter, soy sauce, agave nectar, fish sauce, rice vinegar and chili garlic sauce. Whisk until smooth.
